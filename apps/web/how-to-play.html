How to Play RoboCat: Neon Dash

Master the cyberpunk rooftops with tight controls and upgrades.

Controls

  • Jump: Space / Tap
  • Double Jump: Double‑tap
  • Dash: Shift or swipe right
  • Pounce: Down arrow or swipe down

Tips

  • Use dash to cross long gaps and reset aerial position.
  • Wall‑slide for a safe descent and fast repositioning.
  • Pounce drones for score bursts and scrap drops.

Upgrades

  • Jump: Higher jump + double jump.
  • Dash: Faster dash speed.
  • Skin: Cosmetic hull upgrades for your robo‑cat.
